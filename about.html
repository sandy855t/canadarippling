About Us

Canada Rippling Information Technology Inc. is a consulting company focused on data center infrastructure, technology supply chains, and international business development. The company works with industry partners and clients across North America and Asia-Pacific, supporting technology infrastructure projects and business growth initiatives.

With experience in data center design, manufacturing, and prefabricated modular data center solutions, Canada Rippling collaborates with a wide range of suppliers, engineering partners, and technology providers throughout the data center ecosystem. Our services include data center advisory, supply chain support, vendor representation, and business development.

Our goal is to help organizations identify opportunities, build effective partnerships, and support long-term growth in the rapidly evolving digital infrastructure sector.
